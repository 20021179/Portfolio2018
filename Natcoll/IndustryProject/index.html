Introduction
This is a brief insight into what this private organization is about. Hef was basically intended to be set up as a small business intended for giving advice and seeing to clients concerns as regards to the safety and maintenance of dangerous goods.
H.e.f. haznoinspection services limited was first approved as a small private owned business , set up by (ex osh) explosives and dangerous goods inspector Harry Flannigan.
When It Started
The company was set up mid june 2004 Harry Flannigan was approved by 'ERMA NZ as a Test Certifier and is also contracted by The Department of labour as a Enforcement Officer to carry out inspection work, involving the storage of hazardous substances in work places.
