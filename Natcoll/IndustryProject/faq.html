Hazardous Substances
A hazardous substance is any substance that has the potential to harm the health of people or is a substance listed in the National Occupational Health and Safety Commission's List of Designated Hazardous Substances , or one classified as hazardous by the manufacturer of the substance
Dangerous Goods
Dangerous Goods legislation exists in all jurisdictions in New Zealand. Compliance is required with regard to the storage, handling, packaging and transportation of these goods in accordance with the National Code of Practice for the Transportation of Dangerous Goods by Rail or Road.
