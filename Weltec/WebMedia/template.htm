Welcome

This is a brief insight into what this private organization is about. Hef was basically intended to be set up as a small business intended for giving advice and seeing to clients concerns as regards to the safety and maintenance of dangerous goods. H.e.f. haznoinspection services limited was first approved as a small private owned business , set up by (ex osh) explosives and dangerous goods inspector Harry Flannigan. When It Started The company was set up mid june 2004 Harry Flannigan was approved by 'ERMA NZ as a Test Certifier and is also contracted by The Department of labour as a Enforcement Officer to carry out inspection work, involving the storage of hazardous substances in work places.

 

Brief History

The back ground of 'Hef Hazno Inspection Services goes as far back, as the early days of being a senior inspector at the Wellington City Council. This all sprang back in June 1989 to 2000, when Senior Inspector H Flannigan was solidly grounded and experienced in the field of hazardous substances and dangerous goods.

After his departure at the W.C.C. as chief inspector Osh had offered to hire and employ H.Flannigan as a full time member of this government department, in which he worked for 4 years prior to his retirement.
