How It Was Constructed

The whole idea of the winston peters theme, was inspired by the local news of
him receiving a free meal. Me and a fellow student decided to do a skit on old
whiney himself and do a few rounds, around the local restaurants and cafes.


The Editing Process


Firstly the captured footage was taken from the digital camera, by using a fire
wire and connecting it up to the usb and rendering it into premiere as a avi.

Then it was complimented with subtitles that were constructed in both maya and 

premiere. The maya text was converted into taga files and finally exported as an

avi file. The compressed version that is viewed in this page was compressed in

media cleaner pro as a cinnepack file and later on converted into a asf (mpeg 4)

for the web.
